Understanding and Combating Violence Against Women and Children

What is RA9262 or the Anti-Violence Against Women and Their Children Act of 2004? It is a law the seeks to address the prevalence of violence against women and their children (VAWC) by their intimate partners like their husband or ex-husband, live-in partner or former live-in partner, boyfriend/girlfriend or ex-boyfriend/ex-girlfriend, dating partner or former dating partner.

Comprehensive Learning

Access in-depth courses, workshops, and materials covering VAWC prevention, legal rights, and support strategies. Our curriculum includes:

  • Understanding VAWC types and forms
  • Legal framework and rights
  • Prevention strategies
  • Support system navigation

Professional Support

Connect with certified counselors, support groups, and mental health professionals for confidential assistance. Our support network includes:

  • Licensed therapists
  • Peer support groups
  • Family counseling
  • Recovery programs

Prevention Training

Learn practical strategies for identifying warning signs, intervention techniques, and creating safe spaces. Training modules cover:

  • Early warning signs
  • Safe intervention methods
  • Community protection
  • Emergency protocols

24/7 Emergency Support

Immediate access to emergency hotlines, crisis intervention services, and local support networks. Our emergency services include:

  • 24/7 crisis hotline
  • Emergency shelter referrals
  • Legal assistance
  • Medical support
