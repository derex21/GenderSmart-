GAD Timpala Awards

Celebrating student excellence and dedication in gender and development learning through our recognition program.

Leaderboard Champion

Awarded to students who consistently maintain the highest position in the leaderboard.

  • Consistent top 3 ranking for 3 consecutive months
  • High completion rate of activities
  • Excellent performance in assessments

Early Bird Achiever

Recognizing students who consistently complete activities ahead of deadlines.

  • Consistently early submission of activities
  • Maintains high quality in submissions
  • Active participation in discussions

Perfect Completion Master

Honoring students who achieve perfect scores in their activities.

  • Perfect scores in multiple activities
  • Consistent high-quality submissions
  • Active engagement in learning materials

Community Engagement Star

Awarded to students who actively contribute to the learning community.

  • Active participation in discussions
  • Helping fellow students
  • Contributing valuable insights

How to Earn Awards

Students can earn these prestigious awards by:

  • Completing all assigned activities with high quality
  • Maintaining consistent performance in the leaderboard
  • Submitting activities before deadlines
  • Actively participating in discussions and community activities
  • Achieving high scores in assessments and quizzes

Awards are updated monthly and displayed on your student profile. Keep up the great work!
